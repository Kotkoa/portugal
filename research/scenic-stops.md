# Scenic Stops & Viewpoints

## Along the southern route: Pego → Leiria

### 1. Embalse de García Sola (Reservoir)
- **Coords:** 39.168, -5.218
- **Rating:** 4.8★ (62 reviews)
- **Section:** N-430, between Manzanares and Herrera del Duque
- **Why:** Huge reservoir among Sierra de los Canchales mountains. Road runs along the shore — pull over for mountain lake photos. No detour needed.

### 2. Castillo de Herrera del Duque
- **Coords:** 39.161, -5.034
- **Rating:** 4.3★ (168 reviews)
- **Section:** N-430, Herrera del Duque (same as charging stop)
- **Why:** Medieval castle on a hill above the town. Visible from the road. Kid-friendly! Can combine with charging stop.

### 3. Acueducto de los Milagros (Mérida)
- **Coords:** 38.924, -6.348
- **Rating:** 4.7★ (10,342 reviews)
- **Section:** Mérida, on route
- **Why:** 25m high Roman aqueduct ruins. Free access 24/7. Beautiful reflection in water. 5-min photo stop. Can combine with charging in Mérida.

### 4. Amoreira Aqueduct (Elvas)
- **Coords:** 38.878, -7.172
- **Rating:** 4.7★ (1,866 reviews)
- **Section:** Entering Elvas, on route
- **Why:** Largest aqueduct on the Iberian Peninsula (7 km long, up to 31m high). Visible from the road entering Elvas.

### 5. Forte de Nossa Senhora da Graça (Elvas)
- **Coords:** 38.895, -7.164
- **Rating:** 4.6★ (4,703 reviews)
- **Section:** Elvas, 5 min from route
- **Why:** Pentagonal 18th-century fort on a hill. UNESCO World Heritage. Panoramic views of Spain AND Portugal. Open Tue-Sun 10:00-17:00.

### 6. Pousada Castelo Estremoz
- **Coords:** 38.842, -7.592
- **Rating:** 4.4★ (4,971 reviews)
- **Section:** Estremoz, on the A6/IP7 route
- **Why:** 13th-century castle, now a pousada. Free access to castle area. Views of Alentejo plains with white villages.

### 7. Miradouro de S. Bento (Santarém)
- **Coords:** 39.239, -8.677
- **Rating:** 4.5★ (1,092 reviews)
- **Section:** Santarém, on IC3/A13 route
- **Why:** Panoramic view of Tagus river valley from a high cliff. Cafes nearby. Good lunch stop.

### 8. Ponte da Ajuda (Optional — scenic border crossing)
- **Coords:** 38.777, -7.171
- **Rating:** 4.6★ (571 reviews)
- **Section:** Between Badajoz and Elvas, ~15 min south detour
- **Why:** Ruins of 16th-century bridge over Guadiana river on Spain-Portugal border. Dramatic photos! Only if time allows.

## Border Crossing Recommendation
**Best option: Enter Portugal through Elvas** (UNESCO garrison town). Stop for:
1. Amoreira Aqueduct (visible from road)
2. Forte da Graça (panoramic views of both countries)
3. Elvas Castle (if kid likes castles)

Adds only 15-20 minutes vs highway. Much more scenic than Badajoz-Caia motorway crossing.
